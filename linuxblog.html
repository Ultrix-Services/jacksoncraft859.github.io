What is Linux?

What is Linux? Linux is an Open Source Operating System.
If you don't know what an operating system is, Think MacOS, Windows, Android. Linux can do everything that Windows or MacOS can, but better and faster. Although people can make their own Distributions of it.
Linux can also bring new uses to old computers because it is lightweight. What are some Linux Distributions that you can install and run? Go to the Distributions Section of this blog.

My Take on Linux

What's my opinion of Linux? Honestly, Linux is my favorite OS. Linux allows me to bring new life into old computers and it's much faster than Windows or MacOS. It's also open Source, allowing me to very easily edit and modify it without the restrictions that MacOS and Windows has, while still keeping a ton of features. The only Limits there are is your hardware and your imagination.
I can also still run Windows and MacOS Applications even though i'm running linux. The possibilities of Linux are endless. All Distributions of Linux are unique in their own way. That's my opinion, but don't just take my opinion, make your own opinion. If you want to try Linux but don't have another computer to install linux on, Take a look at my Virtual Machine Blog and follow the instructions.

Conclusion

In Conclusion, Linux is a great OS that people can install and use. It's just as good as Windows and MacOS, and better. It's always changing and expanding, and has endless possibilities each unique. I hope this blog is helpful and gave new information or assisted you in someway. Have a good day.
